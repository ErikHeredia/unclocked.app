Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Twenty5 (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles your data when you use the Twenty5 mobile app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any health-related data. Twenty5 does not currently use or request access to Apple Health (HealthKit) or CareKit APIs.

The app stores all workout tracking data locally on your device and, if enabled, securely in your personal iCloud account.

The only information we collect is:

  • Your email address (used for account identification and support)
  • A unique, anonymous user ID tied to your Apple ID (used solely to prevent trial abuse)

How We Use Data

Data is used solely to provide core app functionality, such as saving your workouts and managing your account. We do not share or sell your data with any third parties.

Your Rights

You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the in-app data reset feature (if available).

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@unclocked.app.
