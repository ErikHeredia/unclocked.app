Unclocked Studios Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Unclocked Studios LLC ("we", "our", or "us") handles user data across all of our applications, including twenty5 and any future products we release.

General Information We Collect

  • Email address (used for account identification and support)
  • A unique, anonymous user ID tied to your Apple ID (used solely to prevent trial abuse)

How We Use Data

We use collected information strictly for providing app functionality and improving our services. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

App-Specific Information

twenty5

twenty5 does not collect health-related data. Workout tracking data is stored locally on your device and, if enabled, securely in your personal iCloud account.

Future Apps

Future apps will have their specific practices described here. Any additional data collected will always be disclosed and handled transparently.

Your Rights

You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app or using in-app reset features (if available). For cloud storage (iCloud), you may delete data via your Apple account settings.

Contact

If you have questions, please contact us at support@unclocked.app.
